About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate UI/UX Designer and Full-Stack Developer with a knack for creating engaging and efficient digital experiences. My journey in tech began with a deep curiosity for how people interact with technology, leading me to specialize in crafting intuitive user interfaces and experiences.

On the front-end, I love bringing designs to life using modern frameworks like React.js and styling with Tailwind CSS to build responsive and visually appealing web applications. My design toolkit includes Figma and Adobe XD, where I focus on user research, wireframing, and prototyping to ensure every product is user-centric.

Beyond the pixels, I'm adept at building robust back-end systems using Python and the Django framework. I thrive on solving complex problems and connecting the dots between design, front-end, and back-end to deliver complete, high-quality solutions.
